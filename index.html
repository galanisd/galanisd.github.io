About Me

Currently, from April 2013, I am a Researcher at Institute for Language and Speech Processing where I have been involved in various research projects such as:

  • LDS: European Language Data Space
  • ELG: European Language Grid
  • Apollonis/CLARIN:EL: Apollonis - CLARIN:EL
  • ELRC: European Language Resource Coordination — supporting Multilingual Europe.
  • OpenMinTeD: Open Mining INfrastructure for TExt and Data (Completed 7/2018).
  • FutureTDM: that seeks to improve Text and Data Mining uptake in the EU (Completed 9/2017).
  • CLARIN-EL: Common Language Resources and Technology Infrastructure (Completed 12/2015).
  • QTLaunchpad: A European Commission-funded project aiming to improve translation technology (Completed 06/2014).

Previously, from March 2012 to December 2012, I did my (compulsory) military service in Greek Army.

From June 2006 to February 2012, I worked as a Software Engineer/Researcher at the Natural Language Processing Group of the Department of Informatics in Athens University of Economics and Business (AUEB); the projects in which I was involved are listed below.

  • "A linear programming approach to multi-document text summarization and natural language generation from ontologies". AUEB Basic Research Funding Program (BRFP) project (04/2011-08/2012).
  • Indigo: Interaction with mobile robots that have personalities and support multimodal dialogues. European FP6-IST project (02/2007-01/2010).
  • Xenios: Human-robot interaction using speech processing, natural language generation, and computer vision. National "Information Society" project (06/2006-12/2007).
Also, in parallel, from Nov. 2006 to Feb. 2012 I was doing my Ph.D. in the same department.

I hold

  • a Ph.D. in Natural Language Processing (title: "Automatic Generation of Natural Language Summaries")
    Nov. 2006 - Feb. 2012, Department of Informatics, Athens University of Economics and Business.
  • an M.Sc. in Computer Science
    2004-2006, Department of Informatics, Athens University of Economics and Business.
  • and a B.Sc. in Informatics
    1999-2004, Department of Informatics, Athens University of Economics and Business.
My research interests include Natural Language Generation, Semantic Web, Question Answering, Sentence Compression, Query-Focused Summarization and Sentiment Analysis.
