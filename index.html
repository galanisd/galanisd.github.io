About Me

Currently, from April 2013, I am a Software Engineer/Researcher at Institute of Language and Speech Processing where I have been involved in various research projects such as:

  • OpenMinTeD: Open Mining INfrastructure for TExt and Data.
  • CLARIN-EL: Common Language Resources and Technology Infrastructure.
  • QTLaunchpad: A European Commission-funded project aiming to improve translation technology.

Previously (March 2012 - December 2012), I did my (compulsory) military service in Greek Army.

From June 2006 to February 2012, I worked as a Software Engineer/Researcher at the Natural Language Processing Group of the Department of Informatics in Athens University of Economics and Business (AUEB) in the following projects:

  • "A linear programming approach to multi-document text summarization and natural language generation from ontologies". AUEB Basic Research Funding Program (BRFP) project (04/2011-08/2012).
  • Indigo: Interaction with mobile robots that have personalities and support multimodal dialogues. European FP6-IST project (02/2007-01/2010).
  • Xenios: Human-robot interaction using speech processing, natural language generation, and computer vision. National "Information Society" project (06/2006-12/2007).

I hold

  • a Ph.D. in Natural Language Processing (title: "Automatic Generation of Natural Language Summaries")
    Nov. 2006 - Feb. 2012, Department of Informatics, Athens University of Economomics and Business.
  • an M.Sc. in Computer Science
    2004-2006, Department of Informatics, Athens University of Economomics and Business.
  • and a B.Sc. in Informatics
    1999-2004, Department of Informatics, Athens University of Economomics and Business.
My research interests include Natural Language Generation, Semantic Web, Question Answering, Sentence Compression, Query-Focused Summarization and Sentiment Analysis.
